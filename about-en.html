About Us


Our Story

We are Mellink, founded in 2016, by a group of young women passionate about fashion and attentive to details. Our foresight is reflected in our unwavering dedication to exceptional apparel accessories, constantly pursuing exquisite craftsmanship and a keen insight into details. Rooted in the vibrant city of Shanghai, our mission is to create outstanding apparel and accessories that embody the essence of modern elegance.

“Outstanding service and reasonable prices, satisfying customers.”

- Satisfied Customer

Our Mission

Mellink's designs cleverly blend contemporary style with the essence of Shanghai's rich culture. Each accessory tells a story, expressing passion, precision, and the harmonious blend of this city where modernity and tradition intertwine. We embrace the fusion of fashion and culture, dancing in harmony with Mellink's unique creations. Our mission is to weave a captivating tapestry at the intersection of fashion and culture through these exquisite designs.
